```markdown
## Decision: [Topic]

**Date:** [date]
**Status:** Decided | Under discussion | Superseded

### Context
[Why this decision was needed]

### Options Considered
1. **[Option A]**
   - Pros: [list]
   - Cons: [list]

2. **[Option B]**
   - Pros: [list]
   - Cons: [list]

### Decision
We chose **[Option X]** because [reasoning].

### Consequences
- [What this enables]
- [What this limits]
- [What we'll need to watch for]
```
